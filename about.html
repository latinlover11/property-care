Who We Are

Reliable & Honest Property Care

10+
Years of Care

Property Care started with a simple belief: that honest work and attention to detail still matter. What began as weekend landscaping projects for neighbors in Longmont has grown into a full-service property maintenance company trusted across four counties.

We're not a franchise — we're your neighbors. Every project, from a simple mow to a complete hardscape build, gets the same care we'd put into our own yards. No cutting corners, no surprise charges, just reliable work done right.

Our Story

From weekend projects to full-time care

2014

Founded in Longmont

Started with a truck, a mower, and a handful of lawn-care clients in the neighborhood.

2017

Expanded to Boulder

Added hardscaping and fence services. Grew to a full-time crew of three serving two counties.

2021

Full-Service Operation

Launched exterior care, carpentry, and handyman divisions. Now covering four counties.

Still Growing
